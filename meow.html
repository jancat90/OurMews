Cat Info

Cats can live from 12 to 18 years! No wonder your interested!
A clicker is used for training a cat.
You click when a cat does something right,
so keep doing it to train your cat!

Top Tip: DONT FEED CATS ANY MILK,CATS ARE LACTOSE INTOLERENT!!

Make sure you don't feed cats any milk because
they have a hard time digesting milk.

Recomended food bowls are stainless steel, or even food puzzles, helps keep them fit!
